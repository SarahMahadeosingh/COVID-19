FAQs




Covid-19 is an infectious disease caused by a new breed of coronavirus. "Covid" stands for Corona Virus Disease.

The most common symptoms of Covid-19 are fever, dry cough and tiredness. Symptoms are said to appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus.

Covid-19 is spread from person to person through respiratory droplets(coughing,sneezing,etc).

There is currently no cure for Covid-19. However, self care is reccommended as a means of treatment and management of the virus.

You can protect yourself from the virus by ridding your body of any outside germs frequently. This is done by washing your hands, eliminating contact between your hands and face and by limiting contact with persons. A mask should also be worn when in public.

The virus is capable of living on objects and surfaces. A study showed that the virus can remain viable on stainless steel and plastic for up to 2-3 days.

According to WHO, 3.4% of reported cases of Covid-19 have died.
